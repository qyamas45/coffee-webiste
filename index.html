Welcome to Bean & Brew Coffee

Nestled in the heart of the neighborhood, Bean & Brew Coffee is more than just a café — it’s a community. Founded in 2018 with a passion for quality coffee and warm conversation, we’ve grown into a cozy hub for students, creatives, and coffee lovers of all kinds.

Our beans are ethically sourced from small farms around the globe and roasted locally to bring out the rich, bold flavors in every cup. Whether you’re here for your morning espresso or a relaxing afternoon with friends, we’re proud to serve comfort, connection, and craft — one cup at a time.

What Makes Us Special:

  • ☕ Locally roasted, fair-trade coffee
  • 🥐 Fresh-baked pastries made in-house
  • 🌿 Sustainable practices & compostable packaging
  • 🧡 A welcoming space for all

Crafted with Purpose

We craft every cup with intention—from sourcing sustainable, single-origin beans to perfecting each pour. Brew Haven is your neighborhood stop for rich espresso, smooth lattes, and small-batch pastries made fresh daily. Good coffee, served honestly.
